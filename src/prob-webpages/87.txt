                 Prime Minister Narendra Modi has called a high level meeting of ministers and top government officials at 3 pm today in wake of the earthquake that shook Nepal and Northern India.               




 


 
 
 
 Prime Minister Narendra Modi has called a high level meeting of ministers and top government officials at 3 pm today in wake of the earthquake that shook Nepal and Northern India.
	According to reports, disaster management mechanisms in the districts of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh along the Nepal border have been activated on the instructions of the Prime Minister. The Prime Minister has also instructed Minister of State for Skill Development Rajiv Pratap Rudy to stock of the situation.
PM has convened a high level meeting with Ministers & top officials at 3 PM in the wake of the situation arising due to the Earthquake.
	— PMO India (@PMOIndia) April 25, 2015

Due to the situation after the Earthquake the PM has spoken to Madhya Pradesh CM Shri @ChouhanShivraj ji.
	— PMO India (@PMOIndia) April 25, 2015

	According to reports, the epicentre of the tremors was said to be in Pokhra, Nepal, with the European Mediterranean Seismological Center stating that the earthquake measured 7.4 on the Richter scale.
