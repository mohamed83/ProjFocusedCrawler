                UP Board (UPMSP) of High School and Intermediate education will be announcing High School (Class 10th ) Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad Results 2015 and UP Board Intermediate (Class 12th) Results 2015 today i.e. on 17th May, 2015 at 12:30 PM on UPMSP Board official Website upmsp.nic.in, upresults.nic.in and upmsp.org               




 
    
          UP Board 10th 12th Results 2015    
          
 
 
 
Uttar Pradesh Board will declare UP Board high school class 10th results 2015, Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad Results 2015 and UP Board Intermediate Class 12th Results 2015 on UPMSP Board official Website on Sunday, i.e. on 17th May, 2015 at 12:30 PM.
The exam was conducted across Uttar Pradesh at various exam centers. The student will need to submit their roll number and sate of birth on the results page to obtain their results.
The results will be available on the UPMSP Board official Website - upmsp.nic.in, upresults.nic.in and upmsp.org
The result would also be available on the www.jagranjosh.com/results.
Students need to log on to any one of the website to access their result. They can take a print-out of the result for future reference. 
 About Uttar Pradesh (UP) Board of High School (Class 10 th) and Intermediate (Class 12th) Education:
Uttar Pradesh Board (UP Board) of High School and Intermediate Education was established in 1921 at Allahabad by an act of United Provinces Legislative Council.
UP Board is the foremost Board in India which had adopted 10+2 structure of examination. The primary examination after 10 years wisdom of High School (Class 10th X) Examination and after the 10+2 / Class 12th examination, there comes after that level i.e. Intermediate Examination
Up board conducted its first examination in 1923. Former to 1923, University of Allahabad was the examining body of the two examinations i.e.  High School Class 10 and Intermediate Class 12.
At this time, UP Board (UPMSP) is the leading examining body in the globe. There are four provincial Offices of the UP Board i.e. Meerut, Varansai, Bareilly and Allahabad.
The formations of the boards were done in the respective years i.e. Meerut was established in 1973, Varanasi was established in 1978, Bareilly was established in 1981 and then the Allahabad was established in 1987. Currently, the UP Board is conducting the high school and intermediate examinations and preparing the Up board results of nearly (30 – 40) lakh students.
The major role of UP Board is to provide endowment to aspiring school, to set down courses and books for High school (Class X) and Intermediate (Class XII) students. UPMSP conducts High school and Intermediate Examination, to grant equality to the examinations organised by other Boards. At present, 9121 secondary schools are renowned by the UP Board of High School and Intermediate Education.
Uttar Pradesh Board (UPMSP) of High School (Class X) and Intermediate (Class XII) Education Examination and Results:
Uttar Pradesh Board of High School (Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad) & Intermediate Education conducts two main examinations i.e. UP Board 10th X Exam also known as High School Exam and Madhyamik Shiksha Paridhad Examination and UP Class 12th Board Exam also known as Inter Exam, 10 +2 Exam and Intermediate Exams.
 
	In 2014, approx 31.27 lakh students appeared for UP Board 12th examination and approx 71.20 lakh students have appeared for the UP Board 10th examination.  This year also, numerous students appeared for the 10th and 12th examination 2015. This year the 2015 exam was completed on March 11th.
	 
	Last year i.e. in 2014 the UP Board 10th Result was declared on 30th May and the 12th class results was also declared by the end of the May 2014. However, this year the UP board (UPMSP) will be announcing their UP Board Results 2015 i.e. UP Board 10th X Class Result 2015 also known as UP Board High School Results 2015 & Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad Results 2015 and UP Board 12th XII Class Result 2015 also known as UP Board Intermediate Results 2015 and UP Board Inter Results 2015 today on 17th May’2015 on UP Board official Website upresults.nic.in, upmsp.nic.in and upmsp.org at 12:30 PM.
