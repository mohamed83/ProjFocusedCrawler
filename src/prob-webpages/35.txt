          BSEAP Class 10th X Results 2015    
          
 
 
 
Today is the day when Class 10 students of Andhra Pradesh will be able to finally get the result of the examination they undertook in the month of March this year.
The Board of Secondary Education of Andhra Pradesh (BSEAP) is going to declare the results on the May 20.
Situated in the capital of Andhra Pradesh, Hyderabad, the BSEAP conducts SSC/OSSC exams twice in a year, and the vocational SSC once a year.
Students can check their results on official website of board http://bseap.org. Or they can log on to http://manabadi.co.in/ to enrol for a free mobile alert.
Candidates will be able to take the print out of their results for future reference once they submit their roll numbers.
Over 13 lakh students appeared for the exam conducted by The Board of Secondary Education of Andhra Pradesh in the month of March.
In addition to SSC exam board also conducts following minor exams - 

1st Year D. Ed Public Examinations
2nd year D. Ed Public Examinations
Pre Primary Teacher Training Certificate ( PPTTC) Examinations
Certificate in Library Sciences (C.Li.Sc) Examinations
Head Masters Accounts Test
Telugu Pandit Training Examinations
Urdu Pandit Training Examinations
Hindi Pandit Training Examinations
Sanskrit Entrance Examination
Professional Advancement Test for Teachers
National Talent Search Examinations
Technical TTC Examination
Technical Certificate Course Examination
National Means Cum Merit Scholarship Examinations.

We wish all the students awaiting their results all the best for the future and we request parents to support their children regardless of the outcome.
