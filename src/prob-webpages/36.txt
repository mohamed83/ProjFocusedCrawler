                The Telangana Board will declare the Class 10th SSC Main and Supplementary examination results today at 11:00 am.
               




 
    
          BSE Telangana SSC Result 2015    
          
 
 
 
The Telangana Board will declare the Class 10th SSC Main and Supplementary examination results today at 11:00 am.
The exam was conducted across Telangana at various exam centers by The Board of Secondary Education in Telangana State.
The student will need to submit their roll number and sate of birth on the results page to obtain their results.
The results will be available on bsetelangana.org and bse.telangana.gov.in.The result would also be available on the www.jagranjosh.com/results.
 Students need to log on to any one of the website to access their result. They can take a print-out of the result for future reference. 
The Board of Intermediate Education was established to regulate and supervise the system of Intermediate education in the state of Telangana and to specify the courses of study and matters connected their with.
