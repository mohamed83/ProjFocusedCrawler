          KSEEB PUC Results 2015    
          
 
 
 
The Karnataka board results for Pre University Education (PUE) 2015, also known as the Karnataka II PU results will be declared today at 11:00 am
More than 6.1 lakh students had appeared in Science, Commerce and Arts streams. While 3.1 lakh students were boys, 4.96 lakh students were girls. 82,067 were repeaters.
Students can check the result by visiting official website kseeb.kar.nic.in and karresults.nic.in. The results would also be available on the www.jagranjosh.com/results.
Students need to log on to any one of the website to access their result. 
Students will need to submit their roll number and sate of birth on the results page to obtain their results.
They can take a print-out of the result for future reference. 
Karnataka Secondary Education Examination Board which came into existence in the year 1966, has been conducting SSLC and other examinations ever since.
Good Luck Students!
