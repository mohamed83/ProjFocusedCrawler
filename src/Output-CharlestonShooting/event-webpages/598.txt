Police: Murder suspect still at-large


 Tyjuan Snipes


ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – Detectives with the St. Louis Metropolitan Police Department are asking for the public’s help in locating a murder suspect.
The St. Louis Circuit Attorney’s Office recently charged Tyjuan Snipes with first-degree murder and armed criminal action.
Snipes is believed to be armed and dangerous.
Anyone with information on Snipes’ whereabouts is asked to call CrimeStoppers at 866-371-TIPS. If your tip leads to an arrest and charges, you may be eligible for a $5,000 cash reward. All calls to CrimeStoppers are anonymous.
