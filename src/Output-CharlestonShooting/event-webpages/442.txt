Police identify victims in double homicide on Lovett Street in Greensboro

Posted 8:43 pm, April 9, 2015, by Web Staff, Updated at 04:24pm, April 10, 2015 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 





GREENSBORO, N.C. — The two men who were fatally shot on Lovett Street Thursday night have been identified as Jesus Baxin-Chigo, 29, and Faustino Chigo-Catemaxca, also 29 years of age.
An acquaintance of the two men discovered them with gunshot wounds inside a vehicle parked in the driveway of Chigo-Catemaxca’s home located at 1500 block of Lovett Street at approximately 8:15 p.m.
Both were transported to Moses Cone Hospital for treatment, but later died from their injuries.
Police are still investigating this incident.
There’s no suspect information at this time. Police said the results of autopsies scheduled for Saturday may provide additional leads in the case.
Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (336) 373-1000 or text the tip to 274637 using the keyword badboyz. All calls to Crime Stoppers are completely anonymous, and may result in a reward up to $2,000.
