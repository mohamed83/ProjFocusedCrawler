Deputy shoots, kills suspect in North Carolina

Posted 5:37 am, April 13, 2015, by Web Staff, Updated at 08:01am, April 13, 2015 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 





















UPDATE — The robbery suspect shot by a  Sampson County sheriff’s deputy has died, according to WTVD.


UPDATE: The robbery suspect shot by a Sampson County sheriff's deputy is dead http://t.co/Le7BL1KKm6
— ABC11 EyewitnessNews (@ABC11_WTVD) April 13, 2015

SALEMBURG, N.C. — A Sampson County sheriff’s deputy has reportedly been involved in a shooting with a robbery suspect near the intersection of N.C. Highway 242 and Reeda Branch Road north of Salemburg.
The deputy spotted the suspect and chased after him. Sources tell WTVD the chase ended where the shooting occurred and the suspect reportedly pointed a shotgun at the officer.
