REIDSVILLE, N.C. — Police are searching for a suspect who shot a man at an apartment complex in Reidsville Thursday morning.


Police got a call about shots fired at an apartment complex located on the 1300 block of Linville Drive at 11 a.m.


Authorities said the victim was rushed to the hospital with very serious injuries. On Thursday afternoon, Reidsville police said the victim has succumbed to their injuries.


The suspect is now on the run.
