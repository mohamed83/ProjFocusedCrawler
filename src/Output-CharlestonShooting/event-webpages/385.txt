1 killed and one injured in shooting in north St. Louis


 


ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Homicide detectives are on the scene of a shooting that injured two man. The shooting happened in the 3800 block of Sullivan Monday afternoon.
One victim was shot in the head and died. The other victim has a gunshot wound to the leg.
