4 shot, 1 dead in North St. Louis shooting

Posted 6:40 pm, June 9, 2015, by Vic Faust 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – St. Louis homicide detectives are investigating a shooting in North St. Louis park. Police say a 29-year-old Jennifer Dorsey was found shot  at North Market and Spring around 4 pm Tuesday. She was pronounced dead a short time later at the hospital.
A 24-year-old an was found with a gunshot wound to the arm nearby at Cote Brilliante and Prairie. He’s listed as stable at a hospital.
Two more men later showed up at a hospital with gunshot wounds. A 20-year-old with a gun shot wound to the leg, listed as being critical/unstable condition and a 46-yera-old with a graze wound to the back, listed in stable condition.
Police say 15 shots were fired at the scene.
Two vehicles seen leaving the scene are wanted in connection to the shooting.
No word yet on any suspects or motive.
