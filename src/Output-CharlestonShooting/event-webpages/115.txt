Greensboro police identify person killed in shooting at apartment complex






 






GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police have identified the person killed at a Greensboro apartment complex Tuesday afternoon as Jermaine Devon Hayes Jr., 19, of 200 Greenbriar Road, 
according to the Greensboro News & Record
.




Hayes was found shot dead in an apartment at 2101 Spencer St. about 5:30 p.m.


Police are still looking for a gray or silver four-door sedan that witnesses saw leave the scene of the deadly shooting.


Capt. Mike Richey said the shooting victim was in an argument with two or three people inside that car. Richey said investigators haven’t been able to determine a race or gender for the people inside the car, but investigators don’t think this was a random shooting.
