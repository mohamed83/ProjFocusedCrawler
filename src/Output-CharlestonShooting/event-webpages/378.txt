Police respond to shooting in north St. Louis


 


ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – St. Louis homicide detectives have been call to Hall and Gimblin in north St. Louis for a double shooting. Police say both victims are males shot in a vehicle on Gimblin. One of the victims is in critical unstable condition, suffering from gunshot wounds to the chest, abdomen, and arms. He was transported to a hospital for treatment. The second victim was transported to the hospital by a family member prior to the arrival of police. He is in stable condition with wound to his arm.
An investigation is ongoing.
