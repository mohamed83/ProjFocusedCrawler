Man dead after shooting in north St. Louis

Posted 6:13 pm, May 25, 2015, by Staff Writer 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 



ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – A 21-year-old man is dead after a shooting in the 2100 block of Breman in north St. Louis.  The shooting occurred jut before noon.
Homicide detectives were called to the scene where the victim was pronounced dead.
No other information is available at this point but we will continue to follow that story.
