Police in Greensboro investigate overnight shooting






 






GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police are investigating after one person was shot in the 3000 block of Greensboro early Saturday morning.


Police said they responded to a shooting reported at 3006 East Market Street at about 3:47 a.m.


Officers found a gunshot victim who was taken to a hospital, but the condition and identity of the victim has not been released.


The suspect is described as a black male, about 5’7” with a large build with a light complexion. Police said he was last seen wearing a scarf on his head and a white shirt.


The investigation is ongoing. Anyone with any information can call Greensboro police at (336) 373-1000.
