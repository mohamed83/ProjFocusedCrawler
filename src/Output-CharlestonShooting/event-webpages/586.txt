Police seeking public’s help identifying Greensboro larceny suspect

Posted 3:52 pm, May 19, 2015, by Web Staff 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 



GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police are asking for the public’s help in identifying a suspect involved in a larceny from a store at 3738 Battleground Ave. in Greensboro.
The crime occurred on April 16.
The suspect stole a necklace and then fled the scene in a gold Chevrolet Impala.
Anyone who knows the identity of this suspect or any information regarding the incident is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (336) 373-1000 OR TEXT keyword BADBOYZ and a tip to 274637. All calls to Crime Stoppers are anonymous.
Greensboro/Guilford Crime Stoppers will pay a cash reward of up to $2,000 for information leading to the arrest or indictment of  the individuals responsible.
