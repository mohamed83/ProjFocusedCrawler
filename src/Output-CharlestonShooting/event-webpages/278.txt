Police request help finding sedan after fatal shooting in Greensboro






 






GREENSBORO, N.C. — The silver sedan seen leaving the area of Spencer Street 
shortly after a homicide
 Tuesday afternoon may play a key factor in the investigation.


Police are searching for a silver four-door sedan, which is believed to have a North Carolina license plate, Capt. Mike Richey 
told the Greensboro News & Record
. Three or four people were inside the car.


It’s wanted in connection with the 
shooting death of Jermaine Devon Hayes Jr.
, 19, of Greenbriar Road. Hayes was found dead in an apartment at 2101 Spencer St. about 5:30 p.m. Tuesday.


Richey said Hayes was at the apartment to visit friends or family.


Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (336) 373-1000.
