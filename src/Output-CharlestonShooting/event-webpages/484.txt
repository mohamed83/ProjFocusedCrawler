Police Need Help Identifying Suspected Tagger


 


STOCKTON-
The Stockton Police Department is looking for the help identifying the individual pictured.
He is suspected of tagging the word “Earl” on numerous buildings in the downtown area.
(Courtesy: Stockton Police Department)
Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (209) 937-8377 or text “Crimes” (274637) and type the word TIPSPD and then their tip.  Tipsters may remain anonymous.
Crime Stoppers pays cash rewards of up to $10,000.
