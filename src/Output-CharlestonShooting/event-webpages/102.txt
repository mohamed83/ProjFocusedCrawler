Police searching for 2 suspects in Greensboro shooting






 






GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police are investigating a shooting that happened late Sunday night in Greensboro.


Officers found a gunshot victim at a home located at 3637 Flint Street around 10:30 p.m. The victim was taken to the hospital with life-threatening injuries.


The suspects were described as two men wearing ski-type masks and black shirts.


The investigation is ongoing.


No arrests have been made.
