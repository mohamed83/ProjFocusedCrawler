78 bullet casings found at scene of north city shooting


 


ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI)- At least 78 bullet casings were found at the scene of a shooting in north St. Louis. The shooting occured around 1:15a.m. Tuesday outside of a home on Maple Avenue.
There is no word on a motive or suspects.
