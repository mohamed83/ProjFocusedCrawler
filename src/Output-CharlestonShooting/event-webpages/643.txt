Greensboro man arrested in Fourth of July shooting


 Julius Anderson


GREENSBORO, N.C. – Police have arrested one person in connection to a woman being shot twice at the Deerwood Meadows apartment complex Saturday night.
The victim, Malika Williams, suffered two gunshot wounds and was taken to a local hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, according to police.
Police said that Julius Anderson, 33, was identified as a suspect and arrested in connection to the shooting.
Anderson was charged with assault with a deadly weapon with intent to kill inflicting serious injury, shooting into occupied property and assault on a female, police said.
According to authorities, Anderson was denied bond and is currently being held in the Greensboro Jail.
The investigation is on-going.
