Suspect wanted for shooting man sitting in his car in Brooklyn




Posted 7:21 PM, June 3, 2015, by 
Alyssa Zauderer
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit
Pinterest
Email
Google
 












SHEEPSHEAD BAY, Brooklyn — Police are searching for a suspect involved in a shooting in Brooklyn that injured a man.


According to police, the shooting happened around 2 a.m., on May 26 across from 2007 Emmons Ave.


Police are looking for this man in connection to the shooting. (DCPI)


A 21-year-old man, was sitting in his car when the suspect walked over and fired several shots before running away.


The victim was taken to Lutheran Hospital where he was treated for a gunshot to the arm.


On Wednesday, police released surveillance video and a photo of the suspect.


Anyone with information is asked to call the NYPD Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the 
Crime Stoppers Website
 or texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) and then enter TIP577.
