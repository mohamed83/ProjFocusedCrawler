Police searching for 2 suspects involved in Bronx shooting




Posted 8:41 AM, July 26, 2015, by 
Julia Jacobo
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit
Pinterest
Email
Google
 












HIGBRIDGE, The Bronx — Two suspects are on the loose after shooting a 31-year-old man in the Highbridge section of the Bronx early Saturday.


The victim was standing in front of 1055 University Ave. around 1:30 a.m. when the suspects approached him and started a brief conversation before shooting him in the leg.


The suspects fled the scene and were seen on nearby surveillance. The victim was taken to Bronx-Lebanon Hospital Center in stable condition.


Anyone with information in regards to this incident is asked to call the NYPD’s Crime Stoppers Hotline at 800-577-TIPS. The public can also submit their tips by logging onto the Crime Stoppers website or texting their tips to 274637 (CRIMES) then enter TIP577.

Posted 3:33 pm, June 23, 2015, by Web Staff 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 





SHELBY, N.C. — The dashcam video from the arrest of the man accused of shooting nine people in Charleston was released Tuesday by the Shelby Police Department.
Dylan Storm Roof, the 21-year-old man who police say is responsible for the shooting, was arrested in Shelby, N.C., on Thursday.
Roof was charged with nine counts of murder.
Roof told investigators that he wanted to start a race war.
