Police investigating fatal shooting on east side


 


LAWRENCE (June 1, 2015) – Police were investigating a shooting Monday afternoon in Lawrence.
Police responded to a shots fired call in the 5600 block of Cheval Lane around 12:15 p.m. That’s in the area of 56th Street and Sunnyside Road. When they went to the home, they found a man dead.
Lawrence police are calling this a death investigation right now. A SWAT team was called out to the area as a precaution.
