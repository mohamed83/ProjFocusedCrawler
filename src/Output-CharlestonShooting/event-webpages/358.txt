Police investigate fatal shooting on near northwest side


 


INDIANAPOLIS (April 30, 2015) – Indianapolis Metropolitan police are investigating a fatal shooting.
It happened around 11:30 a.m. in the 1000 block of Eugene Street. That’s between 30th and Clifton streets.
Police said the gunshot was self-inflicted.
