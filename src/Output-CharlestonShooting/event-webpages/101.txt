Police investigating early morning shooting in Greensboro






 






GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police in Greensboro are investigating a shooting early Monday not far from the campus of University of North Carolina-Greensboro.


At 1:14 a.m. police responded to 1021 Gregory St. in reference to a shooting.


Officers located a gunshot victim who was taken to the hospital. The extent of the victims’ injuries is not known at this time.


The name and address of the victim are being withheld temporarily.


Anyone with information is asked to call Crime Stoppers at (336) 373-1000.
