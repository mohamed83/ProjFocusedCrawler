North St. Louis murder suspects shoot victim in the back inside his home

Posted 6:00 pm, April 11, 2015, by Joe Millitzer 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – The search continues for two suspects who killed a man inside his north St. Louis home.
Shortly before 9pm Friday the suspects knocked on 36-year-old Cameron Wilson’s home along Maple avenue just east of Goodfellow. When Wilson let them in, one of the suspects shot Wilson in the back.
Anyone with information about this murder should call Crimestoppers at 866-371- TIPS.
