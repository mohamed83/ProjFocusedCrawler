Police chase ends in north St. Louis

Posted 3:53 pm, April 15, 2015, by Joe Millitzer, Updated at 03:59pm, April 15, 2015 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail

 View Gallery (5 images)

ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police have a suspect in custody after a chase from Venice Illinois to St. Louis Missouri.  The chase started after 3pm Wednesday afternoon.
The police pursuit of the vehicle began in Illinois and ended at Calvary Cemetery in north St. Louis.  Police say the chase wound around the cemetery.  They were eventually able to stop the vehicle.
There is no information about why police were chasing the car at this point.
More details will be posted as this story develops.  Refresh this page for the latest update.
