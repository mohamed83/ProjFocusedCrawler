St. Louis police searching for suspect in North City shooting




Posted 6:45 pm, June 30, 2015, by 
Chris Smith
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit
Pinterest
LinkedIn
Email
 








ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – St. Louis police detectives are investigating a shooting that occurred Monday afternoon. The shooting happened in the 4900 block of Natural Bridge, where officers responding to the shooting found a 26-year-old African-American male in a dumpster suffering from a gunshot wound to the head. The victim was rushed to the hospital and is listed in critical/unstable condition.


Police have released photos of a suspect wanted in connection to the shooting.


Anyone who recognizes this suspect is asked to call CrimeStoppers at 866-371-TIPS (8477).


An investigation is ongoing.
