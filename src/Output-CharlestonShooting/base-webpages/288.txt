Police cancel Endangered Person Advisory for missing 63-year-old man






 






Update: 
Albert Marshall has been located at an area hospital and is safe, according to police.


ST. LOUIS COUNTY, MO (KTVI)- St. Louis county police want you to keep your eyes out for a missing man. They’ve issued an Endangered Person Advisory for 63-year-old Albert Marshall. Mr. Marshall has been missing from his home on Linnell Drive in north county since 6:30p.m. Wednesday. He is five-foot- seven, and 165 pounds.


He’s schizophrenic and has epilepsy. Mr. Marshall has not been taking his medicine for several months.


If you see him call 911 or St. Louis County police.
