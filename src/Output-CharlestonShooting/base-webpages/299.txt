Police investigating two overnight shootings in north St. Louis




Posted 5:53 am, July 30, 2015, by 
Danielle Scruggs
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit
Pinterest
LinkedIn
Email
 












ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI)-  Police are investigating two overnight shootings in north St. Louis. One man was shot in the stomach around 11:30p.m. on Newstead Avenue at Labadie. A few hours later, a man in his 30’s was shot in the chest on Union Boulevard at St. Louis Avenue.


Both victims are hospitalized. Their conditions are unknown at this time.


There is no word on any suspects or a motive.
