Winston-Salem man’s cousin killed in Charleston church shooting


John Hinton/Winston-Salem Journal


Winston-Salem Journal












The cousin of a minister who was among the nine people killed Wednesday in Charleston, S.C., says his family is devastated by the death.




Ward Miller of Winston-Salem said he learned about the death of his cousin, the Rev. Daniel Simmons Sr., at 5 a.m. Thursday. Simmons, 74, was the associate minister at Emmanuel AME Church in Charleston, Miller said.
