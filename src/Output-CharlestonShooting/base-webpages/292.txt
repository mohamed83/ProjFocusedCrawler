ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – The St. Louis police department is investigating a quadruple shooting on the city’s northside. The shooting happened around 8:45 pm near


Police say the four victims include a female with a gunshot wounds to the chest and neck, and another female with a gunshot wound to arm. Two males suffered gunshots; one with a gunshot wound to the head and the other male victim was shot in the shoulder.
