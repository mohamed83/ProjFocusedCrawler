(KPLR) – A pastor from the Baltimore area, a city that is dealing with racial unrest, has been in Ferguson this week trying to build bridges between African-Americans and the police. Nationally know racial expert, Dr. David Anderson’s approach is different.  He says justice is important but the first step should be forgiveness.


All new on News Eleven, Dan Gray has a one on one interview with Doctor Anderson.
