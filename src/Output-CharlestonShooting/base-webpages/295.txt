Police release identity of woman killed in north St. Louis park shooting






 






ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police release the identity of the victim in 
Tuesday’s fatal shooting in a north St. Louis par
k. Police say 27-year-old Jennifer Dorsey Was killed. Her two daughters, who are both toddlers, were with her at the time but they weren’t hurt.


Three other men at Rombold park at the time of the shooting were injured. Surveillance video shows two cars leaving the area after the shooting.
