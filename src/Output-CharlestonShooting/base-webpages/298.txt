Arrest made in north St. Louis County fatal shooting




Posted 9:23 pm, May 16, 2015, by 
Anthony Kiekow
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit
Pinterest
LinkedIn
Email
 











ST. LOUIS COUNTY, MO (KTVI) –A man was shot and killed Saturday night near the intersection of Halls Ferry Road and Hecht.  He was found dead in the parking lot of an apartment complex.

St. Louis County police now report that they have a suspect in custody. Charges are pending.
