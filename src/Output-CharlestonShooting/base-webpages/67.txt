SHELBY, N.C. -- The dashcam video from the arrest of the man accused of shooting nine people in Charleston was released Tuesday by the Shelby Police Department.


Dylan Storm Roof, the 
21-year-old man who police say
 is responsible for the shooting, was arrested in Shelby, N.C., on Thursday.


Roof was charged with nine counts of murder.


Roof told investigators that he wanted to start a race war.
