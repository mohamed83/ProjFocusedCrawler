Roseville Police Need Help Identifying Bank Robbery Suspect

Posted 4:58 PM, May 27, 2015, by Genny McLaren 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


ROSEVILLE-
(Courtesy: Roseville Police)
Roseville police are asking for the public’s help in identifying a suspected bank robber.
A man robbed the U.S. Bank inside the Safeway on Woodcreek Oaks Boulevard at Blue Oaks Boulevard on Wednesday about 2:40 p.m.
Police say he gave the teller a note demanding cash.
The suspect mentioned a gun, but no gun or other weapon was seen. He left with an undisclosed amount of cash.
(Courtesy: Roseville Police)
No suspect vehicle was seen.
No one was hurt in the incident.
If you recognize the suspect, you are urged to call Roseville Crime Stoppers at (916) 783-7867, or text a tip to 274637, starting your tip with the word “ROSEVILLE.”
