Third suspect arrested in O’Fallon MO shooting


 


O’FALLON, MO (KTVI) – Police have arrested the third and final suspect from a Thursday afternoon shooting in O’Fallon, Missouri. Antonio Malik Whitaker was arrested Saturday for the shooting of a 15-year-old on McQuade Drive.
O’Fallon police say the shooting was the result of a bad drug deal.
Whitaker has been charged with first degree assault, armed criminal action, and attempted first degree robbery. His bond has been set at $250,000 cash only.
