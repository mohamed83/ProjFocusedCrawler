1 dead, 2 injured in North County triple shooting

Posted 5:34 am, May 6, 2015, by Danielle Scruggs 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





SPANISH LAKE, MO (KTVI)- One person is dead and two others are injured after a triple shooting overnight in north St. Louis County. The shooting occured around 2a.m. at an apartment complex located off of Suntree at Dunklin Drive in Spanish Lake. Authorities are searching for two gunmen who they believe are responsible.
There is no word on any motive.
More information on this story will posted as it becomes available.
