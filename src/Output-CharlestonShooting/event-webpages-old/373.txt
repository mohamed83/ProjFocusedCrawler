Police identify shooting victim from Friday


 


ST .LOUIS (KTVI) – Police have identified the victim shot and killed Friday along Walton and Delmar. 21-year-old Deandre Hawkins was shot multiple times and was pronounced dead after being rushed to the hospital. Investigators say the suspect is a short black male with a dark complexion, heavy build with dreadlocks.
He was last seen wearing a black shirt and blue jeans.
He sped away from the scene in a small silver vehicle, possibly a Pontiac G-6 with a black front bumper.
Anyone with information should call the St. Louis police.
