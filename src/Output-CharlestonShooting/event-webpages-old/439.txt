Suspect dead following officer-involved shooting in Pulaski


 Stock photo


PULASKI, Va. — A man in Pulaski, Va., is dead after an officer-involved shooting that happened early Tuesday morning, according to WSLS.
The shooting happened at 12:55 a.m. at the Pulaski Self Storage Facility located at 1847 Bob White Blvd.
Police reported that the suspect was taken to the hospital where he later died.
Special Agent Burnett, who was the officer involved in the shooting, was doing a routine business check at the storage facility when he found a suspicious vehicle on the property Tuesday morning.
There is still no word on what led to the shooting. Police are still working to process the scene.
The investigation is ongoing and police told WSLS they hope to release more information later in the day.
