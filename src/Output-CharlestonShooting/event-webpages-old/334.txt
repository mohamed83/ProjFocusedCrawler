Two shootings in south St. Louis Friday night

Posted 10:11 pm, May 8, 2015, by Staff Writer, Updated at 12:57pm, May 9, 2015 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 



Photo Gallery


 View Gallery (2 images)




 


ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) -Police are investigating a pair of shootings in south St. Louis late Friday night. A 22-year-old man was wounded around 9:15 at Osage and Grand. The victim and another man were walking east on Osage after leaving a gas station when noticed a car pass them several times.  During a final pass, the suspect fired shot at the men, striking the one man in the leg.
Police are looking for a black male who was wearing a light shirt and dark-colored baseball cap. He was driving a silver Pontiac of Monte Carlo.
About 15 minutes later, a 19-year-old man was shot in the leg at Minnesota and Itaska. The victim said he was standing in a gangway with two friends when a gold sedan drove by and an someone inside fired shots from the window.
The victim was shot in the upper leg and was rushed to the hospital where he was listed as stable.
