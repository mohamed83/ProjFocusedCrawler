2 injured during drive-by shooting in Kinloch

Posted 6:39 am, May 19, 2015, by Danielle Scruggs 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





KINLOCH, MO (KTVI)- Two people were injured during a late-night shooting in north St. Louis County. It happened around 11p.m. Monday on Schoolway Drive at Mable Avenue in Kinloch. It appeared to be a drive-by shooting. A large crowd later gathered at the location.
St. Louis County Police are leading the investigation.
There is no word on a motive and no arrests have been made.
