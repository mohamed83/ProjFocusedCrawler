Detectives Investigating Shooting at North Highlands Gun Range

Posted 5:24 PM, April 9, 2015, by Genny McLaren, Updated at 05:22pm, April 9, 2015 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


SACRAMENTO-
Detectives are investigating a shooting at a gun range in North Highlands.
According to the Sacramento Sheriff’s Department, a male was shot at The Gun Range on Orange Grove.
Sgt. Lisa Bowman, with the sheriff’s department, said the shooting appears to be a self-inflicted gunshot.
The man has been transported to a local hospital with life-threatening injuries.
Stay with FOX40 and FOX40.com for updates on this developing story.
