Fall is here, and Winter is on its way! With the approach of colder temperatures, comes the need for warmer clothing by children across Western Oregon.


















 




KEZI 9 News, Eugene’s Active 20-30 Club, and Bi-Mart need your help collecting 
Coats For Kids
! Just look through your children’s closet for gently used coats they don’t wear anymore, and drop them off at any of the partnering 
Coats For Kids
 locations in Eugene and Springfield. The jackets will be cleaned and donated to local children who might otherwise be left out in the cold. Clean out your closet and donate your extra coats today. Let’s work together to help kids in need stay warm and dry this Winter!
