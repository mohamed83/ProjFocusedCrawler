Tom Ryan, the council president of the City of Roseburg, said he was in contact with the mayor and city manager, who had gone to Umpqua Community College where the students were giving statements.


“I still don’t know how many are dead,” he said. “We are in the process of finding out. I can tell you that right now the community is in shock. It is a tragedy.”


He said most of the students at the community college live in or around Roseburg, which has a population of 22,000 inside the city limits, rising to 50,000 in the surrounding area.
