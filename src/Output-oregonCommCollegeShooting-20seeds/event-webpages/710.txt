Deciding which university is perfect for you can be a difficult task. While some students are attracted to private schools with very niche offerings, others are drawn to public institutions that are typically more affordable and offer a broad range of extracurricular activities.


Below is a list of the top 10 public schools in the United States for the 2015-16 academic year. These schools provide students with the best possible education without the high price tag of a private education. Students graduate from these schools with the knowledge and skills needed to become successful in their future careers.

Founded in 1845 by Secretary of the Navy George Bancroft, the United States Naval Academy is the top school for students interested in pursuing a leadership position within the United States Navy or Marine Corps. The academy provides students with the opportunity to explore American history, while earning a degree that leads to a commission in the Navy.

The school blends traditional learning techniques with state-of-the-art technology to prepare students for any obstacles they may face in the future. However, this school is very selective, accepting only about 7% of applicants.
