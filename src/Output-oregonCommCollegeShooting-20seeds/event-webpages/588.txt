10. Old Dominion University: Norfolk, VA


Old Dominion University is a good choice for veterans and active duty military as the school is affordable, yet offers a rigorous education. About 3,000 students in attendance at Old Dominion are recipients of the GI Bill, and participates in three federal programs that set standards for veteran education. For those interested in distance learning, Old Dominion also offers a variety of
