Savannah State University student dies in shooting at student union building




Posted 7:18 am, August 28, 2015, by 
 




 




















SAVANNAH, Ga. — A Savannah State University student in Georgia was shot and killed late Thursday in the school’s student union building, a spokeswoman said.


Christopher Starks, a junior from metropolitan Atlanta, died at a local hospital after an altercation at the union, according to Loretta Heyward, a spokeswoman for the school.


No arrests have been made, Heyward said.


University police and the Georgia Bureau of Investigation are handing the case.
