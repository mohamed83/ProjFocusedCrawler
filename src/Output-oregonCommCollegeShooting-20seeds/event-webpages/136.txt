ROSEBURG, Ore. (KOIN) — At least 10 people were killed and 7 others injured in a shooting at Umpqua Community College, Douglas County Sheriff John Hanlin said Thursday evening.


Around 10:40 a.m., local authorities responded to reports of an active shooter on the campus at 1140 Umpqua College Road.




Kortney Moore, a student at Umpqua Community College, said the gunman shot her teacher and asked others in her classroom about their religion before spraying more bullets.
