Officer Shoots, Misses Armed Man












By 
Brian Richardson
 Apr. 7, 2015










































 Photos














 












LEBANON, Ore. -- The Linn County Major Crimes Team is at a home on the 3-thousand block of Stoltz Hill Road after an officer shot his gun at a man. It happened around six Monday night.




 




Deputies and Lebanon officers responded for a welfare check on a person who was reportedly suffering from a medical issue. Oregon State Police say an armed man confronted an officer.


















 




The officer shot at the man, but missed. No one was hurt and the suspect was taken into custody.
