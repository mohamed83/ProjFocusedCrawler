Neighbors React to Cottage Grove Shooting












By 
Chenue Her
 May. 4, 2015










































 Photos














 












COTTAGE GROVE, Ore. -- Cottage Grove residents are wanting more information regarding a fatal shooting that happened Saturday night.




The Lane County Sheriff's Office is continuing to investigate the shooting that killed one man an injured another man.




The shooting happened around 6 p.m. Saturday in the 1600 block of Curry Avenue in Cottage Grove, LCSO said.


















It is still unknown why the shooting happened.
