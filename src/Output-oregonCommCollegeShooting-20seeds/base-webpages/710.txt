Oregon school shooting: Anchors reflect, talk parenting fears


As Tamron Hall, Willie Geist and Al Roker reflect somberly on Thursday's mass shooting, Natalie Morales reports from Roseburg, Oregon, where the entire community is in mourning even as stories of survival and heroism begin to emerge. "This is now becoming almost one of your biggest fears as a parent," Al says, while Tamron asks: Where are the voices in the middle of the gun debate, rather than the extremes?
