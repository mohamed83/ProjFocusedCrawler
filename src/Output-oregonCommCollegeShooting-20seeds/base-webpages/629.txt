Oregon Sheriff: ‘The Shooter Is Deceased’


by 
 | 5:09 pm, October 1st, 2015




video


























































 




Oregon Sheriff 
John Hanlin
 held a press conference this afternoon to confirm that the shooter who 
 and injured others at Umpqua Community College is dead.


Hanlin said that authorities ended up in a shootout, after which the shooter––identified as a 20-year-old male (with no further information about him yet)––was neutralized.










Please enable Javascript to watch.




When asked if that means the shooter’s dead, Hanlin said yes.


Governor 
Kate Brown
 spoke briefly after the sheriff and said that the shooter was 20 years old.
