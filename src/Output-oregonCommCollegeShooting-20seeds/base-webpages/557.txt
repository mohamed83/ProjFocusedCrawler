FBI Investigating Suspicious Letters in Oregon












By 
KEZI 9 News
 Jul. 28, 2015










































 Video




 Photos




















 












 












EUGENE, Ore. -- The FBI and the U.S. Postal Service are investigating suspicious letters sent to county offices around Oregon, including in Lane County.




Oregon State Police said the Lane County Sheriff's Office received a copy of the same letter that was sent out Monday with powder inside.




The FBI said tests have come back negative for any toxic substances.


















Investigators said around 20 letters were delivered, all addressed to Oregon sheriffs or their offices.
