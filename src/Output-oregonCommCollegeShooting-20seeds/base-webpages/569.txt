ROSEBURG, Ore. — Police say they’ve made an arrest in this weekend’s fire at a storage unit in Roseburg.




In connection with the blaze at Diamond Lake Mini Storage that damaged nearly 30 storage units Sunday night.




Donald Valdez, 27, is facing charges of arson, burglary and criminal mischief.
