Names of Men Involved in Deadly Shooting Released












By 
Matt Templeman
 May. 6, 2015










































 Video




 Photos




















 












 












COTTAGE GROVE, Ore. – The Lane County Sheriff’s Office has released the identities of two men involved in a fatal shooting.




The shooting occurred on Saturday, May 2nd in the 16-hundred block of Curry Avenue.




One man died, and a second was injured.  The injured man with the gunshot wound has been identified as Dale Allen Llloyd, 60, of Cottage Grove.


















The deceased man has been identified as John William Sidman, 64, also of Cottage Grove.




Police aren't releasing many details about the circumstances of the shooting, and they are not calling either one of the men a victim or a suspect.




The Lane County Sheriff’s Office is still investigating exactly what happened.
