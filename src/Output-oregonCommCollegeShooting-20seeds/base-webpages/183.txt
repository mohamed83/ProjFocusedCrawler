But messages found on 4chan have a history of being false, misleading and intentionally wrong. 


Unlike other well-known community sites like Reddit, 4chan users are not required to identify themselves, even with a user name. That makes the site a haven for the kind of people who are interested in declaring things anonymously on the Internet. 4chan message threads often disappear after a few days, granting what is said on the site a feeling of impermanence.


Generally, 4chan is a sprawling group of web forums, and much of its community is populated by users with attitudes that run counter to the mainstream. Members tend to be anti-feminist and offensive in the extreme in other ways, slurs may well be as common as greetings there, and claims of responsibility for all manner of mayhem are not uncommon.
