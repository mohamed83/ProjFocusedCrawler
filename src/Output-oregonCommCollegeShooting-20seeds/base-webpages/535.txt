Coos Bay Man Found Dead in Umpqua River












By 
KEZI 9 News
 Jun. 8, 2015










































 Photos














 












DOUGLAS COUNTY, Ore. -- The Douglas County Sheriff's Office is investigating the death of a Coos Bay man whose body was found floating in the Umpqua River about 13 miles east of Reedsport.




Deputies say Dean Montgomery was found without a floatation device. The 61-year-old's 19-foot boat was found beached on the opposite side of the river. The boat was still running and had extensive damage to the prop and transom. 




Authorities say the case is being investigated as an accident.
