Roseburg Hero Alek Skarlatos Joins Dancing with the Stars












By 
KEZI 9 News
 Sep. 2, 2015










































 Video




 Photos




















 












 












NEW YORK, N.Y. -- Roseburg hero Alek Skarlatos has joined ABC's Dancing with the Stars Season 21 cast.




The announcement was made on Good Morning America Wednesday morning.




According to Dancing with the Stars' Twitter account, Skarlatos will dance with pro Lindsay Arnold.


















Skarlatos became known around the world after he and several others stopped an alleged terrorist on a train bound for Paris in August.




Dancing with the Stars kicks off at 8 p.m. Monday, September 14 on ABC.
