Officer Involved Shooting in Eugene












By 
Tamara Feingold
 Mar. 30, 2015










































 Video




 Photos




















 












 












EUGENE, Ore.– The Interagency Deadly Use of Force Investigation Team is investigating an officer involved shooting in Eugene.




The Eugene Police Department says just after 5:00 Monday officers responded to a call about a suicidal person in the Bethel area.




During the police response, an officer shot and killed the person.


















Police are not releasing any other details at this time.
