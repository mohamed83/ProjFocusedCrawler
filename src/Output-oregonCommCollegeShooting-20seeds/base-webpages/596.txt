The Umpqua Community College Shooter Has Reportedly Been ID’d


by 
 | 8:45 pm, October 1st, 2015




























































 




CBS is reporting the name of the Umpqua Community College shooter, as confirmed to them by law enforcement sources:


JUST IN: Umpqua Community College shooter ID'd as 26-year-old Chris Harper Mercer, law enforcement sources confirm to CBS News.


— CBS News (@CBSNews) 




BREAKING. Two law enforcement sources tell 
 the Oregon shooter was 26-year old Chris Harper Mercer.


— Charlie Kaye (@CharlieKayeCBS) 




10 people 
 and 7 were injured when the shooter opened fire on campus earlier today. 


The campus went into lockdown as authorities responded to the active situation. The gunman was killed in a shootout with police, and students and faculty were bused away from campus.
