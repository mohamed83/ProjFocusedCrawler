The nation was stunned Thursday morning by a mass shooting at Umpqua Community College in the Southern Oregon town of Roseburg.


Initial reports say that ten people died and 20 others were injured in the shooting on Thursday morning.  |  


The tragic events were met with an outpouring of grief and support from Oregon’s public officials, hoping to console a state reeling from the violence.
