Christopher Mercer identified as shooter who killed 9 at Oregon college


Christopher Harper Mercer is the man police have identified as the gunman who killed nine people Thursday at an Oregon college. He was eventually killed during a shootout with police and the community is in mourning as the nation agonizes once again over gun violence. TODAY's Natalie Morales reports.
