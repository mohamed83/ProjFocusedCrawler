Mediterranean migrant boats – a humanitarian crisis in pictures









An Italian patrol ship arrived in Malta on Monday with 24 bodies recovered out of hundreds feared drowned after a migrant boat capsized in the Mediterranean. The death toll from Sunday’s shipwreck off the coast of Libya is one of the highest yet. Officials said there had been at least 700 people on board, some reportedly locked in the hold. Another boat carrying migrants ran aground off the Greek island of Rhodes, with authorites saying that at least three people had died
