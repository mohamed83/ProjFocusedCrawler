Greenpeace message ‘Time for Change: the Future is Renewable’ next to the hummingbird geoglyph in Nazca in Peru. Greenpeace activists from Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Spain, Germany, Italy and Austria displayed the message, which can be viewed from the sky, during the climate talks in Lima, to honour the Nazca people, whose ancient geoglyphs are one of the country’s cultural landmarks. The stunt, however, angered the Peruvian government
Photograph: Rodrigo Abd/AP
