Class Suspensions for Tuesday, July 15 Due to Typhoon Glenda

 Phil Trends Bloggers
 July 14, 2014










Class suspensions for Tuesday, July 15, 2014 have been declared in both public and private schools in various areas in Metro Manila and other provinces after the Philippine Atmospheric, Geophysical and Astronomical Services Administration (PAGASA) raised the public storm warning signal No. 1 of Tropical Storm Glenda (International Name: Rammasun).

Below is the list of areas with class suspensions:
All levels

Camarines Sur
Pateros
Rosario, Cavite
San Juan City
Laguna








Pre-school to high school

Angono
Antipolo
Bataan
Cainta
Caloocan
Cardona
Cavite
Laguna
Malabon
Makati
Manila
Mandaluyong
Marikina
Muntinlupa
Navotas
Paranaque
Pasay
Pasig
Quezon City
San Mateo
Taytay
Valenzuela

Pre-school to elementary

Balagtas, Bulacan
Hagonoy, Bulacan
Malolos City, Bulacan
Las Piñas

According to PAGASA, as of 10PM, Tropical Storm Glenda was located at 940 kilometers east of Virac, Catanduanes maintaining its strength with maximum sustained winds of 65 kilometers per hour (KPH) near the gustiness of up to 80 kph.
