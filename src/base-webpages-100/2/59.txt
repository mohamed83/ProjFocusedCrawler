According to PAGASA, typhoon “Ruby” further intensified and slightly slowed down as it continues to move West Northwest towards Eastern Visayas.
It is expected to andfall on Saturday evening over Eastern Samar and Northern Samar and will be associated with strong winds, storm surge (4-5 meters) and heavy-intense rainfall.
