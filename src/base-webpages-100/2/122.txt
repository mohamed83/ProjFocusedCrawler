Ensemble Tropical Rainfall Potential (eTRaP)



The eTRaP is a simple ensemble whose members are the 6-hourly totals from the single-orbit TRaPs.

More information may be found at these links:  eTRaP product information, Digital eTRaP Formats
and Guidance on Interpreting eTRaP Probabilities.
