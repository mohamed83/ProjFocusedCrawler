Valerie Weigmann Wins Miss World Philippines 2014

 Phil Trends Bloggers
 October 13, 2014










Valerie Weigmann was crowned as the winner of the Miss World Philippines 2014 during the coronation night held at the SM Mall of Asia Arena on Sunday night, October 12, 2014.
Valerie Weigmann Wins Miss World Philippines 2014 / Photo from Norman Norman
Aside from winning the most coveted crown, Valerie Weigmann also won twelve (12) special awards including Best in Swimsuit, Best in Long Gown, Best in Fashion Runway, Miss World Camsur, Miss Solaire, Miss Figlia, Jesi Mendez Salon Ambassador, Miss Bench, Miss Reducin, Miss Blue Water Day Spa, Miss Olay and Miss Hana Shampoo.
Left to right: 3rd Princess Nicole Donesa, 2nd Princess Nelda Ibe, Miss World Philippines 2014 Valerie Weigmann, 1st Princess Lorraine Kendrickson and 4th Princess Rachel Peters ( Photo from Norman Norman
Below is the full list of winners in the Miss World 2014:







MISS WORLD PHILIPPINES 2014 WINNERS:

Miss World Philippines 2014: Valerie Weigmann
First Princess: Lorraine Kendrickson
2nd Princess: Nelda Ibe
3rd Pricess: Nicole Donesa
4th Princess: Rachel Peters

SPECIAL AWARDS:

Best in swimsuit (coronation night): Valerie Weigmann
Best in long gown: Valerie Weigmann
Best in fashion runway: Valerie Weigmann
Miss photogenic (coronation night): Nelda Ibe
Miss World Camsur: Valerie Weigmann
Miss photogenic (CamSur): Rachel Peters
Best in swimsuit (CamSur): Rachel Peters
Miss Sports: Lorraine Kendrickson
Miss Friendship: TIE betweenKristine Angeli Estoque and Christine Balaguer
Best in Talent: Jean Marie Feliciano
Miss Sun Cellular: Kimberly Anne Sarreal
Miss Solaire: Valerie Weigmann
Miss Organique: Nelda Ibe
Miss Figlia: Valerie Weigmann
Jesi Mendez Salon Ambassador: Valerie Weigmann
Miss Bench: Valerie Weigmann
Yahoo Ph readers’ choice: Rachel Peters
Miss Reducin: Valerie Weigmann
Miss Blue Water Day Spa: Valerie Weigmann
Miss Olay: Valerie Weigmann
Miss Security Bank Most Alluring: Rachel Peters
Miss Hana Shampoo: Valerie Weigmann

As the winner of the Miss World Philippines 2014, Valerie Weigmann will be representing the country in the Miss World 2014 pageant which will be held in London later this year.
