List of Emergency Numbers and Evacuation Centers for  Super Typhoon Hagupit (Ruby) in Biliran

Current Events December 3, 2014 0 1465 


 
 







BiliranIsland.com Staff
BiliranIsland.com
First Posted 11:30:00 12/03/2014
Last Updatedc 08:10:00 12/05/2014 
Leyteños, Samareños and Biliranons are forewarned to prepare for the coming of Super Typhoon Ruby (Hagupit), forecast to landfall in Eastern Visayas.

Here are the emergency numbers and evacuation center for Typhoon Ruby around Biliran. Please disseminate this info:
List of Emergency Numbers:
PDRRMO - 0906 470 7040
PD PNP - 0906 306 3919
PHO - 0917 631 8479
BPH - 0917 308 5299
PSWDO - 0908 879 6511
BILECO – 0905 255 9119, 500 9744 Facebook: BilecoBiliran Twitter: @bilecocom Email: bileco1937@gmail.com
Almeria – 0916 435 2733 and 0916 520 4412
Biliran – 0920 907 5386 and 09209 100 670
Cabucgayan - 0926 717 0428
Caibiran – 0917 310 9094
Culaba –  0906 696 7067 and 0917 630 4229
Kawayan – 0939 910 2625
Maripipi – 0906 133 3042 and 0915 900 2535
Naval – 0917 308 7374, 0906 2950 569 and 0939 440 7076
MEDIA:
PIA Biliran – 0917 855 6879,
BiliranIsland.com – 0915 796 2324, 0919 269 4562
Evacuation Centers
Almeria – Almeria National High School;
Biliran – Filemon Nieras Elementary School situated in Sitio Omas, Brgy. San Isidro. , Bato Elementary School, NSU-Biliran, Campus, Biliran Central School, Mormons Chuch
Cabucgayan =   the evacuation centers are the church, brgy. hall and schools away from the seashore and landslide areas as well as large houses in the barangays.
Caibiran =  Proper: Manlabang National High School; schools away from the seashore and landslide areas
Culaba – the schools are their evacuation centers;
Kawayan – all schools except near shorelines & landslide prone.
Maripipi –  the evacuation centers are the church and schools away from the seashore and landslide areas as well as large houses in the barangays.
Naval – Municipal Hall and NSU gym;
