Official list of localities: Landslide susceptible municipalities for Typhoon Ruby

Project NOAH / December 4, 2014 


 Follow 
This list of landslide susceptible areas is based on the forecasted accumulated rainfall which exceeds 100 mm in 24 hours during Typhoon Ruby.
There is no list for 6 December 2014 since none of the areas with landslide hazard will experience more than 100mm rainfall on that day.
Updates to this list shall be made available as soon as a new severe weather forecast is made.
