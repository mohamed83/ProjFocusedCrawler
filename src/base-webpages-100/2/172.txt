The Philippine island of Bohol is slowly recovering from a powerful- a 7.2 earthquake in October and then Typhoon Haiyan 3 weeks later, but now they’re keen to welcome tourists.

Tourism Returns to Bohol, Philippines, After Earthquake and Typhoon
It all sounds dreadfully sad, but the resiliency of the locals tell everyone that they need tourists to provide much needed income. Realistically, it’s almost business as usual in Loboc, the river cruise in the shadow of the damaged centuries-old Jesuit church is operating.
