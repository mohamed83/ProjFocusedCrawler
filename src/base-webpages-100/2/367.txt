Super Typhoon Yolanda Storm Surge, New Video Footage

 Phil Trends Bloggers
 November 18, 2013










Bagyong Yolanda with an international name “Haiyan” storm surge caught on video. This is a new video footage recently released and went viral online.
In the video you will see how strong Super Typhoon Yolanda that hit Philippines especially Tacloban City that had been hardly hit.

According to the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council (NDRRMC) there are 3,976 persons had been reported dead and 1,602 still missing as of November 18, 2013.
The Philippine government has been criticized for lack of dissemination of information about Super Typhoon Yolanda especially PAGASA, the weather agency of the country.







Typhoon Yolanda brought massive destruction in properties and thousands of lives have lost.
By the way, the video was taken by Nickson Gensis, employed at Plan Philippines. He took the video on the top floor of a boarding house were they took refuge.
The video taken on November 8 at Hernani, Eastern Samar, he said that he took the video footage while some people in that place were praying.
