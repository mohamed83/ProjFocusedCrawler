Miss World Philippines 2014 Official Candidates Presented to Media – Photos

 Phil Trends Bloggers
 September 22, 2014










The Miss World Philippines 2014 official candidates were presented to the members of the press during the media presentation held on Wednesday, September 17, 2014 at the Eclipse Bar of Solaire Resort & Casino in Pasay City.

The 26 official candidates of the beauty pageant introduce themselves before the media people. One of them will be crowned as the winner during the Miss World Philippines 2014 Grand Coronation Night on October 12 at the SM Mall of Asia Arena.








Below are the 26 official candidates of the Miss World Philippines 2014:

Lorraine Kendrickson
Cyra Guilalas
Alexa Kirby
Priscilla Kimberley dela Cruz
Gazini Christiana Ganados

Ranielah Marie Oval
Grace Ann Bautista
Moriel Olea
Christine Balaguer
Kimberly Anne Sarreal
Nicole Donesa
Theresa Regli
Ina Dominica Guerrero
Kimberly Pajares
Ruffa Nava
Valerie Weignmann
Nelda Ibe
Jean Marie Feliciano
Abegail Galisim
Frances Ruth Constantino
Carina Jennah Aguilar
Eliza Johanna Cruz
Jahziel Pernia

The winner of the Miss World Philippines 2014 will be representing the country in the Miss World 2014 pageant to be held in London on December 14.
