Justin Bieber Give Love, Give Back Philippines Tacloban Concert Re-Airs on TV5 (January 4, 2014)

 Phil Trends Bloggers
 January 4, 2014










The TV5 network will air a replay of Justin Bieber Tacloban Concert this Saturday at 6:00 in the evening.
This is to fulfil the request of Bieber’s fans and the general audiences who would like to watch again Bieber’s concert in Tacloban.

The international superstar touched the lives of Yolanda Survivors during his surprise visit in Tacloban.
TV5 special will showcase Justin Bieber’s exclusive video coverage, full length interviews and behind the scene footage’s tonight.







During his visit, the Canadian teen heartthrob brought happiness to the Yolanda survivors; he gave them gifts and even played basketball.
Watch the video:
[youtube]http://youtu.be/LEqj52BQHqU[/youtube]
The singer’s trip to the Philippines was part of his #GiveBackPhilippines global fundraising campaign for Super Typhoon Yolanda survivors.
This time, don’t miss to watch Justin Bieber’s Tacloban Concert “Give Love. Give Back Philippines” which is made possible through the help of Smart and PLDT this Saturday only on TV5 network.
