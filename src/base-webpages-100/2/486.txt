The Voice of the Philippines (The Voice Ph) Metro Manila Audition Schedule and Venue

 Phil Trends Bloggers
 January 19, 2014










The Voice Ph scheduled their audition in Metro Manila on January 25 and 26, 2014 (Saturday and Sunday).
According to the Official Facebook Page of the Voice of the Philippines; “The Voice Philippines for Kids” (8-14 years old) will be conducted on January 25 and for the “The Voice of the Philippines Season 2” (16-years old and above) will be held on January 26, 2014.








All aspiring applicants are required to be present at Resorts World Manila or Lucky China Town Mall with your song that you are going to sing for the audition. They further said that no other requirements are needed.
The Voice Ph Metro Manila audition will kick off at around 8:00 in the morning.
