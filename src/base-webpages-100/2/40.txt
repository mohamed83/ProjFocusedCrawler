Wind Alert Update #03 (As of 4:32 PM Sun Dec 07, 2014)



Typhoon HAGUPIT (RUBY) which is now moving across the Samar and Sibuyan Seas will bring Tropical Storm to Typhoon Force Winds gusting from 75 up to 130 km/hr along Bicol Region incl. Masbate, some portions of Southern Quezon and Oriental Mindoro, Romblon, portions of Northern and Eastern Visayas  today until Monday morning (Dec 8). Hence, the RED and ORANGE Wind Alerts (Severe Weather Warnings) are hoisted along the above-mentioned areas.

Meanwhile, the VIOLET Wind Alert has already been cancelled due to the weakening of the typhoon. 


Weather.com.ph has upgraded its features with a current warning menu. Within this menu there are wind and rain warnings. These warnings will give you color coded alerts depending on its severity. This will show you an overview of the entire Philippine map and quickly determine weather warning alerts. This feature is currently available and ready for use. Description of the above alert colors can be  read here.

To get detailed warning, select a particular weather station on the map and get more information on the warning alert.
