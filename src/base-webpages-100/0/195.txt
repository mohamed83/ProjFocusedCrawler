Reportfrom

Published on
08 Nov 2013












 Download PDF (194.74 KB)

Highlights

Super Typhoon Haiyan made its first landfall in Guiuan, Eastern Samar, with maximum sustained winds of 235 kilometres per hour.
Flash floods, landslides and wind damage are reported in Eastern Samar and Leyte provinces.
Telecommunication and electricity remain interrupted. Air and seaports are closed.
Initial needs assessments are underway prioritizing Shelter, Food, Health, WASH,
Camp Management and Logistics.
