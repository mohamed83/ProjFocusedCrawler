The massive quake, which struck Tuesday, also destroyed more 8,600 homes in Quezon City, according to the National Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Council.The bulk of the casualties were in Bohol province. Bohol residents struggle to recover after quakeIn an update issued Saturday, the council said 583 people were injured and 13 were missing.The quake was centered about 620 kilometers (385 miles) south-southeast of Manila, near Catigbian, and its depth was 20 kilometers (12 miles), according to the U.S. Geological Survey.
