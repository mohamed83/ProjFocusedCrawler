Typhoon Haiyan



Typhoon survivors await aid in Philippines - in pictures









Countries all over the world have pledged relief aid to help support those affected by the typhoon, however damage to airports and roads have made moving aid into the most affected areas very difficult. With dead bodies left out in the open air and very limited food, water and shelter, health concerns are growing
