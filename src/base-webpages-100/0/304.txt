Almost 150,000 people were evacuated from their homes in the Philippines on Wednesday after typhoon Rammasun swept through Manila. Rammasun is the strongest storm to hit the country this year, cutting a path across the main island of Luzon before passing south of the capital on Wednesday, toppling trees and power lines and causing widespread blackouts



Source: ITN
