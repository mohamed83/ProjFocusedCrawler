HACKENSACK, New Jersey –
 Police shot and killed a man Thursday evening at a Hackensack, New Jersey home, according to Bergen County Prosecutor John Molinelli.




Photo Gallery






 View Gallery (2 images)










 






While responding to a domestic disturbance call on the second floor of 185 Johnson Ave, one of the responding officers fired his weapon, fatally shooting 22-year-old Raymond Peralta-Lantigua, according to Molinelli.






shootings by my house???? oh crap!!!!

Investigators recovered the knife Peralta-Lantigua was allegedly wielding at the scene.


What led up to the shooting is still not clear.


The investigation is ongoing.
