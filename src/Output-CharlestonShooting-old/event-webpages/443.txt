Update: Man shot in Greensboro dies, homicide investigation underway

Posted 10:50 pm, April 8, 2015, by Paul Choate and Alix Hines 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 





GREENSBORO, N.C. —  The man shot Wednesday night on Martin Luther King Jr. Drive died from his injuries early Thursday morning.
Greensboro police have reclassified the investigation as a homicide.
An acquaintance discovered Antonio Kuamell King, 24, shot inside his residence at 1104 Martin Luther King Jr. Drive at 10:40 p.m.
King was initially listed in critical but stable condition, but his condition deteriorated and he died from his injuries just after 2:30 a.m. Thursday.
Detectives do not believe the shooting was random or that it was a result of domestic violence.
Police are currently pursuing leads in the case.
Anyone with any information is encouraged to call Crime Stoppers at (336) 373-1000 or text the tip to 274637 using keyword BADBOYZ. All calls to Crime Stoppers are completely anonymous and confidential.
