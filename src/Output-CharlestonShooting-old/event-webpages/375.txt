Police investigating 3rd shooting scene in north St. Louis

Posted 11:14 am, April 30, 2015, by Joe Millitzer and Danielle Scruggs, Updated at 12:36pm, April 30, 2015 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police were called to a  shooting scene Thursday morning on Labadie Avenue in north St. Louis.  The shooting happened two blocks north of another shooting on St. Louis Avenue where a black male sustained multiple gunshot wounds. He was rushed to an area hospital for treatment.
Police have confirmed that the two shootings are related.
