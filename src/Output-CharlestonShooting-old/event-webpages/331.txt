Suspect Hits Sacramento Police Car During Pursuit

Posted 9:43 PM, June 23, 2015, by Genny McLaren and Nicole Comstock 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 





SACRAMENTO —
A suspect is in custody after a lengthy pursuit with Sacramento police on Tuesday night.
Officers began the pursuit after the suspect was involved in a minor hit-and-run crash. While the vehicle was fleeing, it also hit a police car.
Officers were able to stop the suspect using spike strips. The chase ended near Stockton Boulevard and Orange.
The suspect is in custody. Police said they believe the suspect may have been under the influence of alcohol.
No one was injured in the incident.
Stay with FOX40 and FOX40.com for updates on this developing story.
