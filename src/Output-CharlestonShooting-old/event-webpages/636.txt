State police investigate shooting on I-70 ramp


 


ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – The Missouri State Highway Patrol is investigating an interstate shooting.
The shooting occurred Thursday night after 8 p.m. on the westbound ramp from Interstate 70 to southbound I-170.
The victim was struck by gunfire and crashed on the ramp. The driver was taken to a local hospital.
Fox 2 News will have more information on this story as it becomes available.
