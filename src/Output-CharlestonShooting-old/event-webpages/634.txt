Police investigating shooting in Berkeley

Posted 6:00 am, June 9, 2015, by Danielle Scruggs 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





BERKELEY, MO (KTVI)- Police are searching for a gunman who shot one person early Tuesday morning in Berkeley. The shooting happened shortly after midnight near Woodhurst Srive and Hatina.
The victim was transported to a hospital.
There is no word on that person’s condition or a motive.
