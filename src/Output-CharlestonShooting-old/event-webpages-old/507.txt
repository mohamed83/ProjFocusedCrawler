Driver hit by gunfire in south St.Louis shooting

Posted 10:36 pm, April 13, 2015, by Staff Writer 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police are investigating a shooting in south St. Louis.  A man was found shot in the 7500 block of Michigan.
People in one car were shooting at passengers in another car.  The driver was hit. He was able to continue driving a few feet and parked the car.  His condition is not known at this time. He was taken away from the scene alive in an ambulance.
There were several shots fired.  Investigators have several markings where bullets were found at the scene of the shooting.
