Juvenile shooting in O’Fallon, Missouri


 


O’FALLON, MO (KTVI) – O’Fallon police are investigating a suspected drive-by shooting Thursday.
The shooting took place on McQuade Drive before 5:45 p.m.
Authorities said the shooting was not a random act and that the neighborhood shouldn’t be in fear.
The victim, a juvenile, was already at a local hospital when police arrived at the scene. The victim was later transferred to St. Louis Children’s Hospital.
No word on any arrests or suspects.
Fox 2 Now will have more information on this story as it becomes available.
