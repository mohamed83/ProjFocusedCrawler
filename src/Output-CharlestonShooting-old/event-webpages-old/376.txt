Police investigating shooting in north city


 


ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI)- St. Louis City Police are investigating a shooting Wednesday morning in north city. The shooting occured shortly before 9a.m. in the 6000 block of Lucille Avenue. Authorities say multiple victims may be injured.
There is no further information at this time.
FOX 2 will keep you updated as details become available.
