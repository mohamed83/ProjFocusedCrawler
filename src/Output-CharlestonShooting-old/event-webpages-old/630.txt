ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police found a shooting victim on the 1500 block of East Grand. Homicide detectives say the 22-year-old black male victim was found dead in his silver BMW SUV with gunshot wounds to the head and back.
According to our partners at the St. Louis Post-Dispatch, police say the killer may have ambushed the man.
