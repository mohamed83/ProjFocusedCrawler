Dellwood Domino’s shooting investigation

Posted 11:02 am, June 2, 2015, by April Simpson 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





DELLWOOD, MO (KTVI) – Police need your help finding a suspect who allegedly shot a man in front of his children at a Dellwood Domino’s Pizza.
Workers there are now taking precautions to stay safe.
A near fatal shooting happened April 27th in the parking lot of this Domino’s Pizza.
The 38-year-old victim was shot multiple times when trying to buy pizza with is kids.
Tequila Hines was one of the workers who ran to help the victim. “When I came outside he was leaning on his car his finger was shot off….it was scary.”
St. Louis County Police describe the suspect as an African-American male between 20-24 years-old. He’s average height, medium build and was last seen wearing a denim jacket with a unique design on the back. The vehicle he got away in is red four door 2014-2015 Nissan Altima with no front license plate, the vehicle may have an out of state plate or a temporary Missouri tags.
If you have any information, please contact the St. Louis county police or crimestoppers 866-371-8477.
