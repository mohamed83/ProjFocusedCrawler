Police respond to shooting in Dellwood


 Courtesy of the St. Louis Post-Dispatch   


DELLWOOD, MO (KTVI) –  Police in Dellwood are investigating a shooting that occurred in the 9500 block of West Florissant near a liquor store shortly after 9:30 pm. Police say an adult male was shot in the neck.
The man has been transported to the hospital.
FOX2 has a crew on the way to the scene.
More details will be on FOX2 news in the morning.
