Police looking for suspect in Greensboro dollar store robbery

Posted 1:56 pm, June 10, 2015, by Web Staff 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 



GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police are looking for a suspect who robbed a dollar store in Greensboro.
The robbery happened Sunday around 10:15 a.m. at the Family Dollar located at 1920 Coliseum Boulevard.
The suspect was described as a 5-foot-10 man who weighs about 165 pounds.
He was wearing a black shirt, blue jeans and was armed with a handgun.
Anyone who knows the identity of the individual or has any information regarding the incident should call CrimeStoppers at (336) 373-1000 or text the keyword BADBOYZ and a tip to 274637.
All calls to CrimeStoppers are anonymous and Greensboro/Guilford CrimeStoppers is offering a cash reward of up to $2,000 for information leading to the arrest or indictment of the individual responsible.
