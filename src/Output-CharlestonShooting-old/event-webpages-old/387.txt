Police called to North St. Louis for homicide


 


ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – St. Louis city homicide detectives have been called to North St. Louis for the shooting of a 30-year-old man. Police say officers arriving on the scene found the man in 3900 block of Natural Bridge deceased.
An investigation is ongoing.
