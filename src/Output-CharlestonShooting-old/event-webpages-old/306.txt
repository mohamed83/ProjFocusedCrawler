Police: Suspects exchange gunfire from vehicles on I-44 near Jefferson

Posted 2:06 pm, April 20, 2015, by Paul Schankman 

FacebookTwitterRedditPinterestLinkedInEmail 





ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Suspects in two vehicles exchanged gunfire on area highways Monday afternoon, according to St. Louis police.  A portion of westbound I-44 is closed near Jefferson while an investigation is conducted.
Police say a dark colored Chevy Impala and a white newer model Grand Am were the vehicles involved in the shooting.   The Grand Am was located at Jefferson and I-44.  Two men attempted to flee the scene on foot.  Investigators say they have been taken into custody.  Police say they found a gun and marijuana.  The Chevy Impala fled the scene.
The original report of gunfire was near southbound I-55 and westbound I-44.
More details will be posted as this story develops.  Refresh this page for the latest update.
