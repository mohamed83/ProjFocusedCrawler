Police: 4 critically wounded in St. Louis shooting




Posted 7:04 am, May 25, 2015, by 
Associated Press
, 
Updated at 08:29am, May 25, 2015
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit

LinkedIn

ST. LOUIS (AP) – Authorities say four people were shot and critically wounded in an attack in St. Louis.


Police say the attack happened Sunday night along the 2700 block of Dayton Street. The two men and two women who were shot were taken to an area hospital.


A police official who declined to give her name but said she had read the report of the shooting said she had no further information, including any information about the assailant or assailants.
