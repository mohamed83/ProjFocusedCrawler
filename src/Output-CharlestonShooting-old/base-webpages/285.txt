Police on the lookout for missing St. Charles County woman






 






ST. CHARLES COUNTY, MO (KTVI) – The St. Charles County Police Department is looking for a missing woman. 52-year-old Catherine Ogara was last seen at 6 AM April 28TH. Police say she was en route to the home of her boyfriend`s father in Chesterfield.


Police say Ogara was driving a 1996 green Jeep Cherokee with heavy-duty steel bumpers and Missouri license plates WH1-U8E.
