Missing 81-year-old man found alive

Update:
 Missing 81-year-old man.  Vernon August Bowers has been found. He was found off Hog Hollow by railroad track. Police say Bowers has been transported to a hospital.

CHESTERFIELD, MO (KTVI) – Police are looking for a missing 81-year-old man.  Vernon August Bowers was last seen on Tuesday at around 7:30am in the hallway of Garden View nursing home.  A nurse found the window open in the room where he escaped. He is diagnosed with dementia and has trouble speaking.


Bowers is described as a white, male, height 5’8″, 150 pounds, gray hair, blue eyes. He was last seen wearing a blue shirt and dark pants.


Chesterfield police are currently searching the area at pond in a subdivision near Faust Park.  It is not clear if the search near the pond is related to the search for the elderly man.


The state of Missouri has issued an Endangered Silver Advisory for Bowers.  Anyone who may know his location is urged to call Chesterfield Police at 636-537-3000.
