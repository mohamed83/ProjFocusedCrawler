UPDATE: Police have found Angela Donahue.  


Angela Donahue.


WILDWOOD, MO (KTVI) – The St. Louis County Police Department is asking for the public’s help in locating a missing and endangered teenager from Wildwood.


Authorities said 19-year-old Angela Donahue made suicidal comments to a family member and left the residence with an unknown amount of prescription medication.


Police describe Donahue as a Caucasian female, standing 5’4” and weighing approximately 120 pounds, with brown hair and blue eyes, wearing a black t-shirt, dark blue jeans, and carrying a purple backpack.


Anyone with knowledge of Donahue’s whereabouts is asked to contact the St. Louis County Police Department at 314-615-5360.
