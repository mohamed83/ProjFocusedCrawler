Police arrest suspect breaking vehicles windows






 






ST. LOUIS (KTVI) – St. Louis police arrested a male suspect Wednesday night in south St. Louis for breaking a vehicles window on Ivory in south St. Louis.


Police say when officers arrived on the scene the suspect bolted and ran to a residence and started kicking the door. As an officer approached the suspect, a fight ensued and both the suspect and officer fell through the door.


The suspect was taken into custody, after being maced.


Officers and children at the residence were treated at the scene for inhaling mace.
