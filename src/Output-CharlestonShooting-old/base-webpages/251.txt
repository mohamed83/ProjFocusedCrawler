Video of looters hitting store after Ball-Bey shooting released by police

, 
Updated at 05:41pm, August 27, 2015
 




Facebook
Twitter

ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI) – Police have released video of looting after unrest related to the
 officer involved shooting of Mansur Ball-Bey
.


St. Louis City Police say that the front windows of Kings Beauty Supply, STL Custom Designs and Nail Spa were shattered on the night of August 19th. The stores are located at 1330 Aubert in St. Louis.


Police say about 30-40 suspects took cosmetic, hair products, jewelry, clothing and other items from the businesses.


The video above is from the looting at Kings Beauty Supply. Police are hoping that the public can help identify the suspects in the photos below.
