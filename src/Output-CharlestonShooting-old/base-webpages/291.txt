Police investigating shooting in downtown St. Louis




Posted 7:43 am, June 9, 2015, by 
Danielle Scruggs
 




Facebook
Twitter
Reddit
Pinterest
LinkedIn
Email
 












ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI)- Police are investigating a shooting Tuesday morning in downtown St. Louis. Authorities were dispatched to the Pear Tree on Market Street around 7:00a.m. They arrived to find two victims with non-life threatening injuries. Investigators believe the victims were shot along State Street in East Saint Louis.


There is no further information at this time.


FOX 2 will keep you updated as details become available.
