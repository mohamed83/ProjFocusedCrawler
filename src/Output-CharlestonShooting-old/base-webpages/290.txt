ST. LOUIS, MO (KTVI)-Police are investigating a shooting in south St. Louis. It happened around 5a.m. Friday in the 4900 block of Finkman. They are calling this accidental.


There is no further information at this time.


FOX 2 will keep you updated as details become available.
