Army personnel gathered in front of a Nepalese helicopter, whose crew spotted the wreckage of a missing U.S. Marine helicopter, at an army air base in Kathmandu on May 15.
Prakash Mathema/Agence France-Presse/Getty Images


KATHMANDU, Nepal—The bodies of all eight people who were on a U.S. Marine helicopter which crashed have been found, said Nepalese government and military spokesmen.
The bodies of six U.S. Marines and two Nepalese soldiers were found near the crash site, said Lakshmi Prasad Dhakal, the Nepalese home ministry spokesman.
While the cause of death has yet to be determined, “the bodies were all badly burned,” Mr. Dhakal said.Advertisement
