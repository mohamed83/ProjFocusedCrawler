Police arrest suspect accused of murdering man in Sugar House


 Eric Burwell


SALT LAKE CITY – Salt Lake City Police have arrested a man they said they believe was involved in the murder of a man in Sugar House five months ago.
Detectives said 36-year-old Eric Burwell suspected of shooting a man on Jan. 24
That day a driver found 24-year-old Christian McDonald lying in the street with a gunshot wound.
McDonald was taken to the hospital where he later died.
