Police investigating shooting near the Fountain Square area


 


INDIANAPOLIS, Ind. (April 17, 2015)   Shortly after midnight Friday, Indianapolis Police were called to the area of State and Prospect on the report of a person shot. Upon arriving they found one male shot in the lower back.
Police are currently investigating the crime scene.  More information will  be posted as it becomes available.
