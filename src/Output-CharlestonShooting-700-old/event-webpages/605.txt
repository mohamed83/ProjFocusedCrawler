Two in Custody After Shooting in Modesto


 


MODESTO-
Two suspects are now in custody following a shooting in Modesto on Monday afternoon.
The suspects allegedly fired shots from a car near Carver Road and 9th Street about 1 p.m. before leading officers on a chase.
The suspects then abandoned their car near a cemetery on High Street.
A perimeter was set up as police searched area homes for the suspects.
John Muir Elementary School was put on lockdown as a precautionary measure.
The suspects were eventually found a few hours later.
The circumstances surrounding the shooting were not immediately clear.
