Citrus Heights Police Looking for Rape Suspect

Posted 7:15 PM, May 12, 2015, by Genny McLaren 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


CITRUS HEIGHTS-
Detectives from the Citrus Heights Police Department are asking for the public’s help locating a suspect wanted in connection with a 2013 rape.
Lawrence Davis (Courtesy: Citrus Heights Police Dept.)
Detectives are are looking for Lawrence Agusta Davis, 29. He is described as a black male, about 5-feet-9-inches tall, weighing 175 pounds.
Investigators have determined the victim and Davis knew each other at the time of the incident.
Detectives recently acquired evidence that they say confirmed the suspect’s identity and a warrant has been issued for his arrest.
Davis has ties to Sacramento, Oakland, and Richmond as well as the state of Oklahoma.
Anyone with information regarding Davis’ whereabouts is encouraged to call the Citrus Heights Police Department at (916) 727-5500.
