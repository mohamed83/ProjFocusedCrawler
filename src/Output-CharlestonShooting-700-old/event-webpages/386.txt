Police searching for suspect in armed robbery at Greensboro gas station

Posted 8:00 am, June 16, 2015, by Web Staff, Updated at 08:05am, June 16, 2015 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 



GREENSBORO, N.C. — Police are looking for a suspect who robbed a Greensboro gas station at gunpoint Sunday night.
The armed suspect entered the Wilco Hess located at 2005 E. Wendover Ave. Sunday night at approximately 10:30 p.m.
According to police, the suspect took an undisclosed amount of money and cigarettes.
If you know the identity of this individual or have any information regarding this incident call Crime Stoppers at (336) 373-1000 or text the keyword BADBOYZ and your tip to 274637.
All calls to Crime Stoppers are anonymous. Greensboro/Guilford Crime Stoppers is offering a cash reward of up to $2,000 for information leading to the arrest or indictment of the individuals responsible.
