Tracy Police Looking for Robbery Suspect Wearing Fake Beard


 (Courtesy: Tracy Police Dept.) 


TRACY-
Tracy police are looking for a man suspected of robbing a credit union on Monday afternoon.
Police say Uncle Credit Union at 1829 W. 11th St. in Tracy was robbed just after 1 p.m. and the suspect was caught on surveillance cameras.
According to a witness, the beard appeared to be fake.
Anyone with information is encouraged to call Det. Jim Harries at (209) 831-6628.
