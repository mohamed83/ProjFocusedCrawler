Police Need Help IDing Suspects Accused of Stealing Baby Formula

Posted 3:30 PM, June 29, 2015, by Genny McLaren 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


DIXON —
The Dixon Police Department is asking for the public’s help identifying three people suspected of stealing baby formula.
On Sunday, three people entered the Dixon Safeway and selected about $1,000 worth of baby formula. They then left the store without paying, according to police.
Anyone with information about the incident is urged to contact the Dixon Police Department at 707-678-7080.
