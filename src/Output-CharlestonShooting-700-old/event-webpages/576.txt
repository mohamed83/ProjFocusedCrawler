Suspect Arrested in Connection with March Shooting Death


 Semaj Howard (Courtesy: Sacramento Police Dept.) 


SACRAMENTO-
Officers arrested a man in connection with a March shooting death on La Riviera Drive on Thursday.
Semaj Howard was arrested on charges related to the death of Quincy Lacey, who was found shot to death on the 7900 block of La Riviera Drive on March 5.
Officers found Lacey under an overpass in the area with multiple gunshot wounds.
A motive for the shooting has not been released.
Howard was booked into the Sacramento Main Jail.
The investigation is ongoing. Anyone with information regarding the shooting is encouraged to call the Sacramento Police Department at (916) 264-5471.
