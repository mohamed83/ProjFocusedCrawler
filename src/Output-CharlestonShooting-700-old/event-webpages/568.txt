Stockton Police Seek Help Identifying Robbery Suspect


 


STOCKTON —
Stockton police need help identifying an armed robbery suspect.
An employee was working at a car wash on North El Dorado Street near Alpine Avenue on Monday afternoon when a man approached him demanding money from the safe and cash register. The employee gave the suspect the money.
The suspect is described as a Hispanic man in his 30s with a goatee, according to police. He was last seen wearing a black baseball hat, black sunglasses, a grey long-sleeve shirt and red shoes.
He fled the scene in a white Dodge Ram pickup truck, police said.
The Stockton Police Department is encouraging citizens to call if they have information pertaining to this investigation. Callers are asked to call the Stockton Police Department at (209) 937-8377, the Investigations Unit at (209) 937-8323, or Crime Stoppers at (209) 946-0600.
