Modesto Police Identify Suspect in Home Depot Stabbing

Posted 12:53 PM, June 26, 2015, by FOX40 Web Desk 

FacebookTwitterRedditGooglePocketTumblrEmail 


Isaias Becerra (Courtesy: Modesto Police Department)
MODESTO–
Modesto Police have identified a man believed to be involved in a stabbing at a Home Depot store earlier this month.
Investigators said Isaias Becerra and another man stole two, $150 power tool kits and left. When an employee asked to see a receipt, police say Becerra and the other man stabbed the employee and ran off.
Becerra is said to be in the Stockton area.
Police encourage anyone with information on Becerra’s location to call Crime Stoppers at (209) 521-4636. Callers can remain anonymous and may be eligible for a cash reward.
Alexandria Mazerolle filed this report.
