Susie Jackson and her son Walter (Source: Family)
Susie Jackson, 87, was killed in Charleston church shooting (Source: Family)


CLEVELAND, OH (WOIO) -
            St. James AME church members in Cleveland will gather Thursday afternoon to pray for a member and his family. According to St. James AME, Susie Jackson was one of the nine victims shot to death at Emanuel AME Church in Charleston, SC Wednesday night. Mrs. Jackson, whose son is a member of St. James, was 87-years-old.Police say 21-year-old Dylann Storm Roof  attended bible study at the historic black church for about an hour before killing six women and three men, including the church's pastor State Sen. Clementa Pinckney.Roof was arrested Thursday morning after a traffic stop in Shelby, NC. Authorities are investigating the shooting as a hate crime.The special prayer service at 8401 Cedar Ave. is scheduled for 4:30 p.m.Copyright 2015 WOIO. All rights reserved.
