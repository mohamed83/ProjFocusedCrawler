The Most Moving Images From The Charleston Church Shooting Aftermath

Nine people were fatally shot during a Bible study at the historic Emanuel African Methodist Episcopal Church in Charleston, South Carolina, a center of black life and culture. The massacre shocked the community and moved them to prayer.
