Dashcam video shows arrest of Charleston church shooter in Shelby

Posted 3:33 pm, June 23, 2015, by Web Staff 

FacebookTwitterGooglePinterestRedditEmail 





SHELBY, N.C. — The dashcam video from the arrest of the man accused of shooting nine people in Charleston was released Tuesday by the Shelby Police Department.
Dylan Storm Roof, the 21-year-old man who police say is responsible for the shooting, was arrested in Shelby, N.C., on Thursday.
Roof was charged with nine counts of murder.
Roof told investigators that he wanted to start a race war.
Read more on the Charleston church shooting here.
Video courtesy of the Shelby Police Department.
