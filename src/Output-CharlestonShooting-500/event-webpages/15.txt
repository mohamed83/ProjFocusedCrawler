Shooting at Historic Black Church in Charleston, South Carolina


















      
        By Elliot Hannon




























 









The latest on the shooting at a Charleston church coming up on the Channel 2 Action News Nightbeat next pic.twitter.com/VlpsPWHScm— WSB-TV (@wsbtv) June 18, 2015



Police responded to a shooting at a downtown Charleston, South Carolina church on Wednesday night. The details are still a bit sketchy, but there are reports, as of yet unconfirmed, there are multiple fatalities  at the historic black church Emanuel African Methodist Episcopal. The church holds Bible study every Wednesday, according to CNN. Charleston police have identified the suspect as a white male, approximately 21 years old.


Update, 11:45 p.m.: An earlier version of this post cited reports that the shooter had been taken into custody. According to Charleston police, the shooter is still at large.
