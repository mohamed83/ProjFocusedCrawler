1 dead, child injured in Rowan County shooting


 


ROWAN COUNTY, N.C. — One man died and a 4-year-old was hospitalized after a shooting in East Spencer Thursday morning.
Kejuan Dishad Lamont Sweat, 22, was at an apartment complex located at 800 South Long Street at 12:30 a.m. when the shooting happened.
Three children were in the vehicle with Sweat when it was hit by multiple gunshots.
One child suffered a non-fatal gunshot wound. The other two children were not injured.
Officer found Sweat’s vehicle at the intersection of West Henderson Street and Annandale Avenue near Novant Health Rowan Medical Center in Salisbury.
He was taken to the medical center where he later died.
According to police, Sweat lived in the apartment complex where he was shot.
No arrests have been made.
The State Bureau of Investigation is investigating with assistance from Salisbury police and the East Spencer Police Department.
