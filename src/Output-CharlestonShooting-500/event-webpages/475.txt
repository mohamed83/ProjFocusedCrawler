‘You rape women and take over our country’ Charleston church gunman named as Dylann Roof


Nicole Morley for Metro.co.ukThursday 18 Jun 2015 4:25 pm 











Dylann Roof is suspected of the Charleston shooting (Picture: PA)
The man who gunned down nine people during a prayer meeting has been identified as Dylann Roof.
The 21-year-old was named after his uncle Carson Cowels, 56, saw the CCTV footage released by police.
Cowels said that Roof received a gun – a .45 calibre pistol – for his birthday in April.
It’s not known if this was the gun used in the attack which killed nine people at in the Emanuel African Methodist Episcopal Church – one of the oldest historically black churches in America.
Roof was identified by his uncle (Picture: Reuters)
Six women and three men were murdered in the attack, including Reverend Clementa Pinckney.
Roof is believed to have said: ‘You rape our women and you’re taking over our country’ before opening fire on the church congregation.
The house of Roof’s mother is currently surrounded by police, but it’s not yet known if he’s inside.
A Facebook picture shows the hate crime suspect wearing a jacket with the flag of apartheid-era South Africa and white minority rule Rhodesia (now Zimbabwe).
Roof has been arrested for trespassing and a previous conviction on a drugs charge, according to local media.
Roof has been arrested previously for trespassing (Picture: Twitter)
