Fatal shooting on near east side


 


INDIANAPOLIS, Ind. (April 19, 2015) – Police arrived at 525 N. Oxford Street to find a man shot just inside the back door of his home.
EMS pronounced him dead at the scene with an apparent gunshot wound to the head.
It happened just after 2:00 A.M.
Homicide detectives are gathering information. No suspects have located yet.
