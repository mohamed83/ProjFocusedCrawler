2 injured in shooting in Greensboro


 


GREENSBORO, N.C. — Two people were injured in a shooting in Greensboro on Wednesday evening.
Officers responded to the 2200 block of Vanstory Street in reference to an assault call at 6:53 p.m.
Upon arrival, officers located two victims who were suffering from apparent gunshot wounds.
The victims were transported to a local hospital with non-life-threatening injuries, police said.
The suspect vehicle was described as a small silver vehicle traveling on Vanstory Street toward Meadowview Road.
An investigation is ongoing.
